Why we need your help

Our Scout Home needs a new roof

In 2026, we need to renovate the roof of our Scout Home in Völs, Tyrol. This comes with considerable costs for our group.

Our building with its 300 m² roof area serves not only the scouting activities of our local group in Völs, but is also regularly used for regional and national scouting events, leader training courses, and by scout groups from all over Austria for camps – making it an important piece of scouting infrastructure near Innsbruck.

Support us in our endeavour and get yourself an exclusive building block – for your scout home, for yourself at home, or as a gift for your loved ones!
